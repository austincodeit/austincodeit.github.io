Welcome to Austin Code's GitHub Page!

The mission of the City of Austin Code Department is to provide quality education and enforcement of city codes and ordinances so that Austin will be a more livable city.

"Preserving Austin's Quality of Life"

Austin Code is here to keep the community clean and safe. Through the continuous training and an ongoing focus on customer service, consistency, and transparency, violations around the city will be brought into compliance. If compliance isn't met, Austin Code will proceed with enforcement actions in order to make sure the community stays livable for everyone in Austin. Read more...
